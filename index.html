The picture before the facts

The risk is rarely obvious at the beginning.

Clients usually arrive with polished proposals, warm referrals, confident claims, and a sense that the timing is urgent. What's harder to see is what's missing, what's exaggerated, and what cannot be independently checked. That gap is where most avoidable losses are made.

What clients are usually told

  • "The opportunity is time-sensitive."
  • "The documents are already in order."
  • "The partner has done this before."
  • "Everyone involved is reputable."
  • "The risk is low."

What clients actually need to know

  • Who is behind the entity?
  • What claims can be independently checked?
  • What documents are missing or inconsistent?
  • What reputational, legal, or operational red flags exist?
  • What questions should be answered before commitment?
Why KYA Axiom exists

Built to protect good decisions from bad information.

KYA Axiom was created because important decisions are too often made with incomplete, emotional, or unreliable information. We slow the process down just enough to verify what matters, identify what is missing, and give clients a clearer basis for action.

Know Your Advantage means knowing the facts, the risks, and the questions to ask before you commit.

The KYA method

Clarify. Verify. Assess. Advise.

A small, repeatable discipline that runs the same way for a counterparty review and a private opportunity — sized to the decision, not the spectacle.

01 — Clarify

Define the decision.

Define the decision, parties involved, client concerns, available documents, timing, and risk tolerance.

02 — Verify

Check what's checkable.

Review public records, online presence, ownership indicators, documents, media, sanctions or adverse signals where applicable, and the consistency of claims.

03 — Assess

Surface what matters.

Identify red flags, missing evidence, inconsistencies, governance gaps, reputational concerns, and practical exposure.

04 — Advise

Deliver clarity.

Deliver a clear report with findings, risk rating, unanswered questions, and next-step recommendations.

Scope & boundaries

Clear scope. Clean boundaries. Better decisions.

KYA Axiom provides research-led advisory and decision support. We do not make the client's decision, guarantee outcomes, or replace licensed legal, tax, investment, banking, or regulatory advice. When a matter requires a licensed professional, we help clients identify the right questions to bring to that professional.

We do

  • Review information
  • Identify red flags
  • Structure findings
  • Clarify questions
  • Support better decisions

We do not

  • Hold or move client funds
  • Provide escrow
  • Provide legal or tax advice
  • Provide investment advice
  • Perform regulated consumer background checks for employment, housing, credit, insurance, or eligibility decisions
